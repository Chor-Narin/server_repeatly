The Greatest Volunteer Program is officially open. The Applications are now open for the 2026 FIFA World Cup Volunteer Program. One of the biggest and Greatest Volunteer programs in the History of FIFA. The FIFA 2026 World Cup is officially accepting applications. They are looking for 65,000 Volunteers. Open to applicants from around the world. Volunteers must be available from June 11, 2026, to July 19, 2026. You’ll need to be 18 years old at the time of application. This year, the FIFA 2026 World Cup will be held in 16 host cities across the USA, Canada, and Mexico. There is no application fee to apply. Additionally, volunteers are welcome from all backgrounds. Your passion and energy are the most important tools. More details are below.

Host Countries and Cities

On the application form, you will be asked to indicate your preferred Host City. Please be aware that you can only select one Host City, and this selection cannot be changed once submitted.

Canada (2 cities)

  • Toronto (BMO Field / Toronto Stadium)
  • Vancouver (BC Place / Vancouver Stadium)

Mexico (3 cities)

  • Guadalajara (Estadio Akron / Estadio Guadalajara)
  • Mexico City (Estadio Azteca)
  • Monterrey (Estadio BBVA / Estadio Monterrey)

United States (11 cities)

  • Atlanta (Mercedes-Benz Stadium / Atlanta Stadium)
  • Boston (Gillette Stadium / Boston Stadium)
  • Dallas (AT&T Stadium / Dallas Stadium)
  • Houston (NRG Stadium / Houston Stadium)
  • Kansas City (GEHA Field at Arrowhead Stadium / Kansas City Stadium)
  • Los Angeles (SoFi Stadium / Los Angeles Stadium)
  • Miami (Hard Rock Stadium / Miami Stadium)
  • New York / New Jersey (MetLife Stadium / New York New Jersey Stadium) – The final will be played here.
  • Philadelphia (Lincoln Financial Field / Philadelphia Stadium)
  • San Francisco Bay Area (Levi’s Stadium / San Francisco Bay Area Stadium)
  • Seattle (Lumen Field / Seattle Stadium).

Eligibility Criteria

  • The Volunteer application is open to applicants from around the world.
  • Commitment: Eight shifts between 11 June 2026 and 19 July 2026.
  • Age: 18 years old or older at the time of application.
  • Eligibility: Must comply with the entry requirements of the host country.
  • Language: Speak English (additional languages are a plus!).
  • Team Tryouts & Training: Attend Volunteer Team Tryouts and complete training.
  • Verification: Agree to and pass a background review (process varies for Mexico).
  • Please read the FAQs before applying.

How to Apply for the FIFA World Cup 2026 Volunteer Program?

To apply for the volunteer program, first join the FIFA Volunteer Community. Once registered, carefully review the program’s requirements. Then, complete the application form and the online assessment, where you can highlight why you’d be a valuable addition to the volunteer team. The Link to the official website is below.
